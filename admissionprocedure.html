Admission Procedure

Ist Year A pass in +2 Examinations with Physics and mathematics as compulsory subjects along with chemistry or biology or Biotechnology. Direct IInd Year Diploma / B.Sc degree holders are admitted directly to the second year BE courses. Post Graduate Relevant UG Degree.

Admission Criteria

50% seats are earmarked for admission under Government quota by the Commissioner of Entrance Examinations. Admissions to the remaining 50% seats are done by the Management, based on the score at the marks scored for Physics, chemistry and Mathematics at the Plus Two examination.

Applications are also invited for admission to second year of B.E. Degree course, under Lateral Entry Scheme.
