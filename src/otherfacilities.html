Other Facilities

Extracurricular Activities

Each department is having a Student Association managed by students and assisted by staff advisors. These associations conduct seminars, workshops and cultural programs. The following clubs conduct other activities relevant to their areas:

1. Blood Donors club
2. Red Ribbon Club
3. NSS

Our students actively participate in the design, demonstration and execution of several innovative engineering projects that serve the society.

Sports & Games

Due attention will be given to the health and physical fitness of the students. The age old adage that ‘A sound mind in a sound body’ is more relevant in today’s world and heavy stress and high competition.

Internet Connectivity

The college has dedicated high speed internet connectivity through 12 mbps broad band connection. The internet facility is available from 8 A.M to 6 P.M.

12 Mbps BSNL Leased Line

The Internet provided by the Central Computing Center also makes manythings. The students are provided the facility to update their knowledge through the use of Internet. Our college has 12 Mbps BSNL leased line connected to more than 1000 terminals with WI-FI Technology throughout the campus. Student and faculty are free to access internet after the regular working hours. The access speed enables the staff and students to download and upload files at a faster rate.

Class Rooms

The class rooms are all very spacious and the area is much more than the specification of the AICTE. Lecture halls and Laboratories are well connected with Public Address System and facilities are provided for power point presentation.

Store

A stationery store is opened in the college campus, from where students can purchase Text Books and other stationery items for their day-to-day requirements and academic activities.

Canteen

The college canteen is maintained directly by the College management. The canteen adheres to the highest degree of quality, hygiene and provides the students with tasty food at affordable rates. The day scholars can very well utilize the facility.
