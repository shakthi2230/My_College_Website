Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering

Vision & Mission

Vision

Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering shall become the centre of academic excellence in area of Power, Energy conservation and Electric vehicles and the transmitter of moral values with focus on the development of rural masses

Mission

Equip the students with advanced knowledge in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering as well as professional skills necessary to face the challenges of the future

Engineer them to engage in research activities leading to innovative applications of technology for the benefit of mankind

Enable them to become responsible citizens of the country with a willingness to serve the society
